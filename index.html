Direction

Chicken Run!!!

Chicken run is about a chicken that fled from its cage for freedom realized that the best place to live is in the cage. You can take this chicken back home. But try not to get caught by dangerous animals. Users can move the chicken by pressing W(up), A(left), S(back), and D(right). Dangerous animals can appear from left or right with different rate of speeds. Enjoy!!
